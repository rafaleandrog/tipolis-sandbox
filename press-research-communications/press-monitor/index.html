Tipolis Press Monitor

Weekly news pipeline. From RSS to a branded report.

Five operational screens drive the Monday workflow: maintain the search terms, triage the AI-classified results, edit the bullet summary one item at a time, preview the report, generate the Doc, and browse the archived history grouped by country.

Workflow

Pick a screen

Each card opens the screen that handles that step. The Apps Script Web App is the only backend — the Google Sheet stores everything else.

Step 0 · Maintain Search terms Enable, disable, or fine-tune the keywords the daily search uses. Step 1 · Triage Approve or reject Review the classified week and approve the items that go into this week's report. Step 2 · Edit Summary wizard Trim the AI-generated bullets one article at a time. 6–10 → 3–5. Step 3 · Generate Preview & report Reorder by section, generate the Word/Doc copy, then archive the week. Browse History Every archived article grouped by country. Click to drill in.
